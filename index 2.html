The Forge loop

A wrong answer isn't a fail. It's raw material.

Flashcard apps show you the right answer and move on. Forge treats the wrong answer as the most useful data in the session — and works it, there and then.

Corrective starters · Auto-generated Ready to use

Each high-frequency misconception pairs with a ready-made 5-minute starter activity — projected on the board, worked through as a class, targeting the exact error.

MC-INF-01 23 fires this week
Starter: "Is it deflation?"

Project this scenario:

"UK inflation falls from 6.1% to 1.8%. The BBC reports that 'the cost of living crisis is easing.' A student in your class says prices are now falling."

Task (2 mins): On your whiteboard, write whether prices are rising, falling, or stable — and the correct term for what has happened to inflation.

Reveal: Prices are still rising (inflation is positive at 1.8%). The rate has slowed — this is disinflation. Prices would only be falling if inflation were negative (deflation).
