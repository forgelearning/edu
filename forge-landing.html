The Forge loop

A wrong answer isn't a fail. It's raw material.

Flashcard apps show you the right answer and move on. Forge treats the wrong answer as the most useful data in the session — and works it, there and then.

01 · TRIGGER

The misconception fires

Every distractor is written from documented examiner-report errors and tagged to a specific misconception — not "wrong", but why it's wrong.

02 · SCAFFOLD

A 30-second correction

Instead of just revealing the answer, Forge shows a two-sentence scaffold that dismantles that exact logic trap while it's still hot.

03 · RE-FORGE

Prove it, reframed

A twin question tests the same concept in a new scenario, immediately — so the fix is understood, not memorised.
